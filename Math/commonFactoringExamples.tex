\subsection*{Common Factoring Examples}
$$
x^2 - a^2 = (x + a)(x - a)
$$

$$
x^2 + 2ax + a^2 = (x + a)^2
$$

$$
x^2 - 2ax + a^2 = (x - a)^2
$$

$$
x^2 + (a + b)x + ab = (x + a)(x + b)
$$

$$
x^3 + 3ax^2 + 3a^2x + a^3 = (x + a)^3
$$

$$
x^3 + a^3 = (x + a)(x^2 - ax + a^2)
$$

$$
x^3 - a^3 = (x - a)(x^2 + ax + a^2)
$$

$$
x^{2n} - a^{2n} = (x^n - a^n)(x^n + a^n)
$$
