\subsection*{Quadratic Equation}
\noindent For the equation :
$$\boxed{ ax^2 + bx + c = 0 }$$
$$x = \dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

$$\boxed{ x^2 + a_1x + a_2 = 0 }$$

$$x_1 = \dfrac{-a_1 + \sqrt{a_{1}^2 - 4 \cdot a_2}}{2}$$

$$x_2 = \dfrac{-a_1 - \sqrt{a_{1}^2 - 4 \cdot a_2}}{2}$$
